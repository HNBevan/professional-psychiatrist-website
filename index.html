Meet Dr. Vance

With over 15 years of experience in clinical psychiatry, I am dedicated to helping individuals navigate life's challenges. My approach combines modern medical science with compassionate psychotherapy to treat the whole person, not just the symptoms.

I specialize in treating anxiety disorders, depression, and adult ADHD. I believe in a collaborative relationship where we work together to find the best path forward for your mental wellness.

Credentials:
MD, Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine
Board Certified, American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology
