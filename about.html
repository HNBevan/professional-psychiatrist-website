Meet Dr. Elena Vance

A compassionate psychiatrist committed to helping individuals achieve mental wellness and live fulfilling lives.

Dr. Elena Vance is a board-certified psychiatrist with over 15 years of experience in clinical practice. She founded her private practice in London with a vision to provide personalised, patient-centred psychiatric care in a warm and supportive environment.

Her approach combines the latest advances in psychiatric medicine with evidence-based psychotherapy techniques, treating the whole person rather than just the symptoms. Dr. Vance believes in building collaborative relationships with her patients, working together to develop treatment plans that align with their individual goals and values.

Outside of her clinical work, Dr. Vance is passionate about reducing the stigma surrounding mental health and regularly contributes to public education initiatives in the community.
