Our Clinical Services

A detailed look at our evidence-based approaches to mental wellness.

Psychiatric Evaluation

Our initial psychiatric evaluation is a comprehensive and collaborative process. We take the time to understand your unique story, including your personal history, current symptoms, and life circumstances.

This in-depth assessment allows us to form an accurate diagnosis and develop a personalized treatment plan that aligns with your goals. We believe a thorough evaluation is the cornerstone of effective mental health care.

Book an Evaluation

Medication Management

Medication can be a powerful tool in managing mental health conditions, but it requires careful and expert oversight. Our approach to medication management is conservative and evidence-based.

We work closely with you to find the right medication and dosage, prioritizing your comfort and minimizing side effects. Regular follow-up appointments ensure your treatment remains effective and adjusts as your needs evolve.

Discuss Medication

Psychotherapy

Psychotherapy, or 'talk therapy,' provides a confidential and supportive space to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. We specialize in evidence-based modalities such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and mindfulness-based techniques.

Through therapy, you can develop practical coping strategies, gain deeper self-awareness, and build resilience to navigate life's challenges with greater confidence and calm.

Start Therapy

Telepsychiatry

Receive high-quality psychiatric care from the comfort and privacy of your own home. Our secure, HIPAA-compliant telepsychiatry platform makes mental health services more accessible than ever.

Whether you have a busy schedule, mobility challenges, or live in a remote area within the state, our video appointments provide the same level of care and connection as an in-person visit.

Book a Video Session

Couples Counseling

Relationships face challenges, and seeking guidance is a sign of strength. Our couples counseling sessions are designed to improve communication, resolve conflicts, and deepen intimacy.

We provide a neutral and structured environment where both partners can feel heard and understood. Together, we'll work on building a stronger, more resilient partnership based on mutual respect and effective communication strategies.

Book Couples Counseling

Group Therapy

There is immense power in shared experience. Group therapy offers a unique opportunity to connect with others who are facing similar challenges in a safe and professionally-led setting.

Participants gain support, offer encouragement, and learn from one another's perspectives. It's a cost-effective way to foster personal growth, reduce feelings of isolation, and build a strong support network.

Join a Group
